$$xy + \overline{x}z + yz = xy + \overline{x}z$$
### Dimostrazione
$$xy+\overline{x}z+yz=xy+\overline{x}z+yz\cdot1=xy+\overline{x}z+yz(x+\overline{x})=$$
$$=xy+\overline{x}z+xyz+\overline{x}yz=xy\cancel{(1+z)}+\overline{x}z\cancel{(1+y)}=xy+\overline{x}z$$
